# Version History

## 2024-04-01

Original firmware, as shipped on all Mark I units when sold

## 2024-07-28 

Updated firmware with slight improvements to filtering and hysteresis,
to prevent "phantom updates" when using very long USB cables, or operating
in an environment with lots of electromagnetic interference.

If you are experiencing lots of "phantom updates" try applying the following firmware:
"Firmware-Mark1.2024-07-28-highthreshold.uf2"
